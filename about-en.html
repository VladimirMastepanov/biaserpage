About

An audio tool. Nothing more.

"Biaser exists to make sound — biased, saturated, alive. No subscriptions, no ads, no internet required."

I. What It Does

Biaser turns your device into a small, self-contained noise instrument. Press play and it generates audio — no setup, no presets to download, no waiting.

II. Why It's Offline

An audio tool doesn't need the internet to make sound, so Biaser doesn't ask for it. No accounts, no servers, no subscriptions — it simply runs.

III. Your Data

Biaser collects nothing and tracks nothing. The full details live in the Privacy Policy.
