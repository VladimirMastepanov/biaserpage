Biaser

privacy policy

Filed — June 2026

"Biaser is an isolated audio instrument. It exists to make sound — nothing else. No accounts, no tracking, no noise from the modern internet."

1 No Data Collection

Biaser does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, device identifiers, or usage analytics. What happens on your machine stays on your machine.

2 Fully Offline

The app needs no network connection to run. It works entirely offline — zero bytes of data ever leave your device.

3 No Permissions Requested

Biaser does not access your microphone, camera, location, contacts, or files. It only generates audio.

4 Contact

For questions, bug reports, or feedback, write to mastepanovmail@gmail.com.
