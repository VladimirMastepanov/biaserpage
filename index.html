"Frustrator is an isolated audio entity. It exists solely to generate sound, free from the noise of the modern internet, tracking, and data harvesting."

01. ZERO DATA HARVESTING

The Frustrator app operates in absolute isolation. It does not collect, store, track, or leak any personal data, device identifiers, or analytics. Your machine remains yours.

02. ABSOLUTE OFFLINE MODE

The system requires no network connection. It works entirely offline, meaning zero bytes of data are transmitted to third-party servers or external networks.

03. NO PERMISSIONS REQUIRED

Frustrator does not access your microphone, location, camera, contacts, or internal storage. It generates raw audio without invading your hardware privacy.
